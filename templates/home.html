PetScan

PetScan is a tool that allows users to extract data, generate lists of Wikipedia (and related projects) pages or Wikidata items that match certain criteria, such as all pages in a certain category, or all items with a certain property - PetScan on Meta-Wiki.

Quarry

Quarry is a public querying interface for Wiki Replicas and ToolsDBs. SPARQL Bridge uses the results of previously executed queries from Quarry as they were at the time of execution - Quarry on Meta-Wiki.

Incubator

Wikimedia Incubator hosts test wikis for projects and languages that do not yet have their own production sites. SPARQL Bridge can expose Incubator pages that already have Wikidata sitelinks, so they can be queried alongside WDQS data.

New Pages

This source exposes newly created pages from one or more Wikimedia wikis as RDF. It validates selected hostnames against SiteMatrix and returns schema.org sitelink-style triples that work well in federated SPARQL queries.
